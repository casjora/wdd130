frequently Asked Questions

Which is superior, a laptop or a PC? Q

+

When it comes down to choosing between a desktop and laptop, if you don't need portability, are on a budget and want the ability to upgrade later, a desktop PC is the better choice. Particularly if you're a gamer or professional in need of a powerful PC, you can't go wrong with a desktop computer.A

How do I troubleshoot a slow computer? Q

+

Check for background processes, run antivirus scans, and ensure your hard drive has sufficient free space.A

What should I do if my laptop won't turn on? Q

+

Ensure the battery is charged, try a different power outlet, and if needed, perform a hard reset.A

How can I protect my computer from viruses and malware? Q

+

Install reputable antivirus software, keep your operating system updated, and avoid clicking on suspicious links.A

How often should I back up my data, and what's the best method to do so ? Q

+

Regularly back up important data. Use external hard drives, cloud services, or automated backup software.A
